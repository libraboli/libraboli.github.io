Bo Li(李博)

I received the BSc and PhD degrees from the Department of Computer Science, Nanjing University, China, in 2014 and 2019, respectively. From 2020 to 2023, I served as Senior Researcher of Youtu Lab in Tencent, China. I am currently a Lead Researcher in the image algorithm research Center of vivo. Our group is the core algorithm team responsible for advancing the photographic quality in the flagship smartphones with the cutting-edge technologies (low-level vision, MLLM, AIGC, etc).
