Computer Vision · MLLM · AI Imaging

libra@vivo.com

I lead research at vivo BlueImage Lab, where we work on mobile imaging, computational photography, content generation, visual agents, and foundation models. My current work connects academic research in vision and generative models with camera, album, and on-device imaging systems.

Before vivo, I was a researcher at Tencent Youtu Lab. I received my B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees from Nanjing University. My research has appeared in CVPR, NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML, ICCV, ECCV, IJCV, TPAMI, AAAI, and ACM Multimedia, spanning AI imaging, dense prediction, camouflaged object detection, federated learning, adversarial robustness, diffusion models, and multimodal vision.

I also serve the research community as a Senior Area Chair for NeurIPS and Area Chair for ICLR. Public profiles: Google Scholar, OpenReview, DBLP, X.
