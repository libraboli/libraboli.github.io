vivo BlueImage Lab

Public research agenda and project links from vivo Camera Research.

vivo BlueImage Lab explores mobile imaging, computational photography, content generation, agents, and foundation models. The lab's public research presence is visible through the vivo Camera Research GitHub organization, which publishes projects spanning diffusion-based imaging, matting, bokeh rendering, visual grounding, photo editing, and video generation.

vivo's public BlueImage material describes an imaging stack that brings together sensing, algorithm, and imaging-chip technologies, while exploring AIGC and 3D imaging for future mobile imaging experiences.
